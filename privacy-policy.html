Privacy Policy

Last updated: [add date]

This Privacy Policy explains how Kaushik Pal Cabs ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects the information you share with us when you use this website or contact us to book a cab.

Information we collect

When you fill out our booking form or contact us directly, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Phone number
  • Pickup and destination locations
  • Travel date and number of passengers
  • Any notes or preferences you share with us

How we use your information

We use the information you provide only to:

  • Confirm your cab booking and arrange your trip
  • Contact you regarding your booking via call or WhatsApp
  • Provide fare estimates and travel updates
  • Improve our services

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

How your information is shared

Booking details you submit are sent directly to our WhatsApp number for the purpose of confirming your trip. Corporate booking enquiries submitted via email are used solely to respond to your enquiry.

Data retention

We retain booking-related information only as long as necessary to fulfil your trip and for basic record-keeping. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Cookies and analytics

This website uses standard analytics and advertising tools (such as Google Ads and Google Analytics) to understand site traffic and measure the performance of our advertising. These tools may use cookies to collect anonymous usage data.

Your rights

You can contact us at any time to ask what information we hold about you, request a correction, or ask us to delete it.
